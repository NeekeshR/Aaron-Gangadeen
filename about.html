Aaron Gangadeen

Hi! I’m Aaron, a digital and traditional artist. Traditionally, I have worked with and enjoyed several mixed media, including paint, watercolor, markers, pen, charcoal pencils, and wax-based colored pencils. I also profoundly enjoy working digitally, as it creates such a stimulating touch.
I can create both traditional and digital artworks, including portrait artworks, cartoon characters, book illustrations, surrealistic art, fan art, and even landscape art. I’m a winner of several art competitions and a holder of a British High Commission Art Certificate, so you can be assured that your commission work will be in authentic and reliable hands.
I guarantee that I’ll be efficient at communicating and satisfying my customers' preferences within a reasonable time and at a suitable price.
